Privacy Policy for Beadly

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Local documents

Beadly is a document-based app for creating peyote, brick, and loom beading patterns. Your project files are stored on your device unless you choose to export, share, or sync them through Apple services you control.

2. Design data

Pattern maps, palette information, completion state, and document metadata are part of your Beadly files. We do not operate a separate Beadly cloud service for storing that design data.

3. Printing, sharing, and exports

When you export a PDF, print a pattern, or share a document, that action uses Apple system features on your device. The destination of that export depends on the service or app you choose.

4. In-app purchases

If you unlock unlimited colors, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.
